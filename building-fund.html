What Your Gift Supports

Every dollar goes directly toward building a home worthy of our community and our faith.

⛪
Church in the Coptic Tradition
A beautifully designed church building rooted in the ancient Coptic architectural tradition, a sacred space for worship and prayer.
🚗
Underground Car Park
An underground car park, making it easy for our whole community to access services and events.
🏀
Multi-Purpose Courts & Play Area
Multi-purpose sports courts and a children's play area to support youth activities, sport, and healthy community life.
✅

Every donation to the St Wanas Building Fund is fully tax-deductible. Many supporters choose to give monthly or leave a legacy gift in their will — every contribution, large or small, makes a difference.
